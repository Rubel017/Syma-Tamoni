About me

Hello, I'm Sayma Khandaker Tamoni

Nicknames: Buri, Tiu Buri, Buri of the moon

Birthday: 1st January.

First a man then a Muslim, Bengali, I am a simple girl from Barisal. I love to listen to Islamic music. Tears and Laughter is my soul, and sleep is my life. I am very arrogant, angry, and stubborn. I like the river bank and the sea, I haven't seen the mountains yet. I Always find the serenity to stand in the middle of the rain and feel every drop of rain. Walking alone on an empty street gives me a different kind of peace. when I hear the name of traveling, who else gets me? but with only my family members. I like chocolate, fuchka, and cake badly. The smell of flowers, the call of birds, the gentle sun on the green field, the wind, the play of light and shadow, the twilight, the moonlight, the sky full of stars, they are so drawn that I want to get lost far away. My favorite color is white, in which all colors are hidden. My Favorite flower is Belli, Shefali. They have a heavy sweet smell. Very restless and stubborn, angry, arrogant, do what I say. I talk a lot all day and suddenly I sit silent. I am sometimes so surprised by my behavior that I feel very strange. A little too emotional. As I can't bear the pain of others, I can't take my own. I don't like complications at all. Simple, beautiful, I want to live by clinging to purity and once in my life I want to fly like a bird, I want to be a fairy and I want to be a Buri of the moon and bring light to everyone.
